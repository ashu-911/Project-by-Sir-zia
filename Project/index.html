Abstract

This research explores the advancements and capabilities of ChatGPT 4.0, an AI language model developed by OpenAI. ChatGPT 4.0 demonstrates remarkable improvements in contextual understanding, response generation, and reasoning compared to its predecessors. The study focuses on its architecture, fine-tuning methods, safety alignment, and its applications across education, research, and software development. Through this project, we analyze the model’s ability to simulate human-like conversations, generate code, and provide context-aware assistance, showcasing the growing impact of generative AI in modern technology.
